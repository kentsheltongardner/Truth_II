Truth II

Overview

Truth II is a Metroidvania

Gameplay

Exploration, combat, puzzle solving, no cinematics

Controls

Mouse and keyboard, no deviation for consoles or handheld

CT-41

Shields, integrity, battery, generator, tools, nanite colony + adaptation

Movement

Wheels, thrusters, high grip treads, Altars

Combat

Enemies stay dead until time passes

Victory

Plenty of time is given, but the player may squander it if he is not careful

Saving

Tree of life style save, new branch after each load, added to tree when player saves. CT-41 can save whenever after BCU removed

Weapon and Tool Use

Weapon combinations, heavy emphasis on discovering correct weapons or tools to use against specific enemies

Necrolith and Companions

Experience, combos, selection

Interface and Saving

Menu operation, plugins like Optimus and Scribe

Optimization and Materials

Lasers, Destroyer weapons, reservoir weapons

Camera and Vision

Unseen, seen before, seen now

Temporal Manipulation

Compression, Kozlov's Toy, Truth I, II, and III

Dialog

Communication optional, allow complete or partial skipping

Purpose Driven Design

Each area has a reason to be, whether produced my natural forces or rational creatures

Physics

Water, ice, lava, heat, sensors

Visual Style

Art is greater than poly count, hand drawn is greater than photorealistic, distinct areas, but consistent feel

Respect for the Player

Laws for treatment of player

Game Philosophy

Goals for player experience

Themes and Story Arc

Coming into being as a person, family, the other, time
