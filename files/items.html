Items
Overview

During its life on Lumen 2, CT-41 receives items as gifts from members of the Farsight Expedition, retrieves abandoned items from the Farsight Colony and the Paralus, finds Native items from ruins in the Realm, and takes Destroyer items from the Vault and the Destroyer Wreck. When CT-41 finds an unknown item that is not compatible with its design, it must allow a certain amount of time for its Nanite Colony to analyze and adapt the foreign items for its use.

Tools

Tools are items that CT-41 can equip on its right and left arms. Tools come in several different categories, which are discussed below. Many of the puzzles in Truth II can only be solved if CT-41 equips a correct pairing of Tools.

Certain Tools can be upgraded or improved by CT-41 throughout Truth II. All Native Tools can be improved by filling their material storage compartments. When CT-41 completely fills a Native Tool's material chamber, it gains a major improvement and changes appearance.

Destroyer Tools are distinct from Native and human Tools in that they do not drain CT-41's battery as they operate.

Kozlov's Toy is a conceptual prototype, and when he made it Kozlov did not have the materials necessary for its operation. CT-41 finds exotic materials during its journey which allow the Toy to produce four seperate temporal beams.

God items are made from Seeds taken from the Old Gods, and take many years for the Nanite Colony to fashion into the ultimate weapons, utility items, and defensive systems.

Lasers

Lasers are continuous, instantaneously damaging weapons. Lasers can be improved by Optimus, an optimization program written by Otis Cobb and gifted to CT-41. A fully optimized laser deals twice as much damage to enemies as an unoptimized laser. Optimization of lasers occurs more quickly as CT-41 encounters new enemies.

Lasers and laser combinations can be used to interact with Glowstones of different colors.

Thermal Laser

The Thermal Laser is a weak red laser. CT-41 receives the Thermal Laser from Otis Cobb before it begins its brush-clearing mission.

Anti-Personnel Laser

The Anti-Personnel Laser is a green laser. CT-41 finds the Anti-Personnel Laser in the weapon's locker in Sood's quarters.

Military Laser

The Military Laser is a powerful blue laser. CT-41 takes the Military Laser from the Agent.

God's Light

The God's Light is an incredibly powerful white laser. The Nanite Colony adapts the radiant Seed of Chemosh into the God's Light.

Material Removal

Material removal Tools act as keys to certain passages locked by earth, stone, and metal. Each material removal Tool can also act as a weapon, though they are not as effective in battle at other Tools.

Sonic Auger

The Sonic Auger is able to blast apart and dissipate loose debris, soil, and sediments. The Sonic Auger deals slight damage and linear knockback. CT-41 finds the Sonic Auger in the Upper Levels of the Mine. CT-41 must use the Sonic Auger to unearth Abelino Montes' remains, and to enter the Caverns.

Rydberg Matter improves the material removal rate and push-back strength of the Sonic Auger.

Impact Cannon

The Impact Cannon is able to shatter cracked glass and stone. The Impact Cannon deals damage and knockback radiating from the point of impact. CT-41 finds the Impact Cannon in the Ancestral Halls, in the Explorer's Shrine. CT-41 must use the Impact Cannon to enter the Sanctuary.

Stabilized Neutronium improves the impact radius, strength and damage of the Impact Cannon.

Resonator

The Resonator is able to melt thin or corroded metals. The Resonator deals damage at an increasing rate the longer it is trained on a target. CT-41 finds the Resonator at the western end of the Factory.

Quark-Gluon Plasma improves the melting speed, spread rate, and damage ramp of the Resonator.

Native Weapons
Scattershot

The Scattershot is a close range energy weapon once used by Warriors. It fires a high number of energized projectiles at incredible velocities, with additive knockback based on the number of projectiles striking a target. The Scattershot produces recoil, and has a long recharge time. CT-41 finds the Scattershot in a bone pit in the Beasthome.

Pentaquarks increase the shot count and spread of the Scattershot.

Particle Emitter

The Particle Emitter is a piece of scientific equipment CT-41 finds in the Accelerator. It rapidly emits particles that each apply a small knockback to a target. The Particle Emitter also causes CT-41 to experience the highest overall recoil of any Tool.

Free Electrons increase the fire rate and velocity of the Particle Emitter.

Corner Cutter

The Corner Cutter is an Explorer's weapon, taken for self-defense and hunting long journeys into the unknown. Its fast, particulate shots ricochet off walls and most inorganic objects, allowing the skilled Explorer to snipe prey and threats from otherwise impossible vantage points. The Corner Cutter's projectiles decrease in energy after each bounce, before finally impacting a valid target or dissipating. Each time the Corner Cutter's projectile bounces off a wall, it produces a radial shockwave and becomes smaller. CT-41 finds the Corner Cutter west of the Lower Ruins.

Q-Balls increase the initial power of projectiles launched from the Corner Cutter, and thereby increase the number of bounces.

Volt Gun

The Volt Gun is an electrical Tool used to temporarily charge and power machinery and robotics. It has a deleterious effect on most lifeforms, dealing slight damage and stunning them briefly. CT-41 finds the Volt Gun in the Upper Root Fields after defeating the Overgrowth. CT-41 must use the Volt Gun to navigate several areas of the Realm before it restores power by defeating the Vermin Supreme.

Superconductors increase the bounce count, stun duration, and damage that the Volt Gun deals to enemies. They also increase the amount and duration of power provided to machinery.

Tranquilizer

The Tranquilizer is a Tool developed by Farmers and Warriors to sedate Natives and other Lumene creatures. Once hit by a pulse from the Tranquilizer, a target creature will become unresponsive for up to several seconds, depending on its size, or until attacked. The Tranquilizer only affects creatures that possess a Lumene Seed. CT-41 finds the Tranquilizer in the Menagerie.

Photonic Molecules increase the stun duration of the Tranquilizer.

Energy Siphon

The Energy Siphon is a beam that dissipates an object's molecular energy, pushing it toward extreme low temperature. If it is trained on a target long enough it will cause it to freeze, at which point the object can be shattered by a sufficiently energetic impact. The Energy Siphon increases CT-41's energy if trained on a living target. CT-41 finds the Energy Siphon in a cryogenics lab in the Refuge.

Bose-Einstein Condensates increase the freeze rate of the Heat Sink.

Medusa

The Medusa is a devastating experimental weapon created by Native Engineers. When fired at a target, and depending on the target's mass, the Medusa petrifies the target completely. The Medusa uses an enormous amount of energy, and has no effect on a target if the energy required to petrify the target is more than CT-41 can store in its battery. If CT-41 misses a valid target, its battery will be drained completely. CT-41 finds the Medusa in the Flow Caverns.

Degenerate Matter increases the upper mass limit for ossification by the Medusa.

Criticality Lance

The Criticality Lance is a powerful penetrating beam adapted from the body of a Magma Worm adolescent. The weapon glows blue as CT-41 powers it up, and discharges an incredibly powerful gamma ray burst that penetrates matter. The weapon only fires for an instant, but leaves visible plasma or heated material along its path. The Criticality Lance can be powered past a threshold at which it begins to produce dangerous levels of ambient radiation, damaging CT-41. The damage dealt to CT-41 increases as the Lance is charged up past the threshold.

Actinides increase the charge rate of the Criticality Lance.

Other Tools
Flashlight

The Flashlight illuminates a broad cone in front of CT-41. Nicholas Ormeil left the Flashlight and his head in the cave where the Beast cornered and killed him.

Turbine

The Turbine is a mariner's Tool that pulls the user forward through the water. CT-41 finds the Turbine in the Pump Station after it equips the Hydraulic Pistons from the Hall of Relics. CT-41 must use the Turbine (or the Hydraulic Pistons alone) to enter the Domains of Thosmodeme's Fragments.

Superfluid increases the impulse applied to CT-41 by the Turbine.

Strange Attractor

The Strange Attractor is a Tool developed by Native Engineers which creates an attracting force between CT-41 and a target. The magnitudes of the impulses generated by the Strange Attractor are based on the inverse square of the distance between CT-41 and the target.

Gravitons increase the magnitudes of the symmetrical impulses generated by the Strange Attractor.

Divining Rod

The Divining Rod is a thief's Tool that detects abnormal Materials in a cone by flashing. The Divining Rod flashes most rapidly when a Material is close to the Rod, and near the center of the cone. CT-41 finds the Divining Rod in the Prospecting Camp at the top-western end of the Badlands.

Neutrinos increase the effective width of the Divining Rod's search cone.

Mass Inhibitor

The Mass Inhibitor is a work Tool once used by Laborers and other Natives to help transport heavy loads. CT-41 finds it in the Quarry behind an enormous cut stone.

Higgs Bosons reduces the energy requirements of the Mass Inhibitor.

Tunneler

The Tunneler is a personal version of the Paralus' Tunnel Drive. It allows a user to jump across space, through barriers and obstacles, at the cost of enormous amounts of energy. Space visibly curves while the Tunneler's mass accumulator rapidly powers up for the jump, and then quickly returns to its normal curvature after the jump is complete. CT-41 finds the Tunneler while fleeing from Voronash aboard the Paralus. CT-41 must have the Tunneler before moving up through the Openings or into the Nucleus through the most difficult stretch of the Razor Caverns. The Tunneler does not allow CT-41 to teleport into liquids. The Tunneler comes with a circular indicator which shows the maximal jump distance for CT-41's center of mass.

Native Prosthetics

The Native Prosthetics are a unique pair of Native items that CT-41 finds in a prosthetics lab in the Refuge. Unlike all other items, the Prosthetics can only be equipped simultaneously. When equipped, they allow CT-41 to interface with Terminals, and control advanced Native machinery and robotics, such as mining equipment, sentry turrets, fans, and more. Unlike other Native Tools, the prosthetics cannot be upgraded by Materials.

In order to control Native machinery, CT-41 must move next to terminal and equip the Prosthetics, and then left-click on the Terminal. The Prosthetics extend to interface, and CT-41 controls present or remote technology until it right-clicks. CT-41 cannot communicate with the Deliverer or the Intelligence without the Prosthetics.

Destroyer Weapons
Coronal Aperture

The Coronal Aperture is a Destroyer weapon that opens to spew out coronal plasma and radiation. Like all Destroyer weapons, the Coronal Aperture does not require energy from CT-41's battery to fire. CT-41 takes the Coronal Aperture from the Torchbearer at at the center of the Darkstar.

Volatility Launcher

The Volatility Launcher is a Destroyer weapon which CT-41 takes from the Enforcer in the Restricted Area of the Hearth. The Launcher opens a tunnel that admits a Volatility, a bouncing, semi-stable anti-matter grenade designed to detonate when triggered by the Launcher, or after a certain time elapses. The Launcher propels the Volatility at a speed determined by the amount of time CT-41 charges the device. The device charges rapidly, firing at a minimum velocity when not charged and at a maximum velocity after charging for 1 second.

When the Volatility detonates, it deals large damage and knockback. Unlike most other weapons, the Volatility Launcher deals heavy damage to CT-41 if misused. As a Destroyer weapon, the Launcher does not take energy to fire. The anti-matter grenade hurled by the Launcher is a physical object, and can be manipulated by physical and temporal effects.

Kozlov's Toy

Kozlov's Toy is a conceptual prototype built by Vadim Kozlov. At the time of the Toy's manufacture, Kozlov did not have the materials necessary for its operation. CT-41 finds exotic materials during its journey which allow the Toy to fire four separate beams. As the Toy fires at an object, it drains energy based on the object's mass. CT-41 finds Kozlov's Toy in Kozlov's Lab. CT-41 is unable to enter Thosmodeme's Temple until it has found Kozlov's Toy and all of the Materials necessary to enable its first three beams.

Kozlov's Toy operates in one of four separate modes, rather than as four separate Tools, to prevent the use of two time manipulation beams at the same time. When different Materials are equipped in the Toy, pulses will move quickly or slowly down the beam, there will be no pulses, or pulses will move backward.

Slow

Kozlov's Toy requires Strange Matter to generate a Slow Beam. This beam causes an object to move at half its normal speed. After all physical calculations are complete, the Slow Beam simply halves the update velocity when deciding on a resulting position. CT-41 finds the Strange Matter after defeating Fesh'Togar.

Speed

Kozlov's Toy requires Hyperons to generate a Speed Beam. This beam causes an object to move at double its normal speed. After all physical calculations are complete, the Speed Beam simply doubles the update velocity when deciding on a resulting position. CT-41 finds the Hyperons after defeating Azeselon.

Stop

Kozlov's Toy requires Quantum Spin Liquid to generate a Stop Beam. This beam causes an object to freeze in the moment of the present. The object has infinite mass and does not update its position. CT-41 finds the Quantum Spin Liquid after defeating Uruthmu.

Switch

Kozlov's Toy requires Time Crystals to generate a Switch Beam. This beam causes an object to move backward in time. While moving backward in time the object has infinite mass, and a velocity opposite that of when it moved forward in time.

Shields

Shields absorb damage taken by CT-41, preventing losses of integrity. Shields only begin to recharge when CT-41's battery is full, and when it is not taking damage.

Energy Shroud

The Energy Shroud is a weak personal protection device worn by a Native killed during the Destruction. The Nanite Colony adapts the device for CT-41's use after CT-41 finds it in the Desolation.

Warrior's Guard

The Warrior's Shield is a shield CT-41 takes from the battleground inside the Assembly Hall.

Thermal Barrier

The Thermal Barrier is a powerful shield designed to be worn by workers in the Mineral Separation Plant. CT-41 finds and adapts the Thermal Barrier as it moves toward Azeselon.

Challenger's Shield

The Radiation Buffer is an incredibly powerful shield CT-41 takes from destroyed Challenger. If CT-41 fights with great skill, it may acquire this shield early in its journey.

God's Veil

The God's Veil is a maximally powerful luminous shield. The Nanite Colony adapts the shimmering Seed of Molech into the God's Veil.

Armor

Armor platings increase CT-41's integrity. Each armor material is adapted into plating by the Nanite Colony. CT-41 must acquire certain armor upgrades before it can pass through several highly damaging episodes of its journey.

Aluminum Paneling

The Aluminum Paneling is the Caretaker model's standard armor.

Steel Cladding

The Steel Cladding is armor which the Nanite Colony adapts for CT-41 from the Steel Stock found in the Machine Shop.

Inconel Plating

The Inconel Plating is a tough armor which the Nanite Colony adapts for CT-41 from the Inconel Billet it finds near the Executor manufacture and supply area of the Fortress.

Titanium Armor

The Titanium Armor is a superior armor which the Nanite Colony adapts for CT-41 from the Grade 23 Titanium Sphere it finds in the Prospecting Camp.

God's Hide

The God's Hide is a maximally strong armor. The Nanite Colony uses material from the nigh indestructible Seed of Dagon to create the God's Hide.

Batteries

Batteries hold CT-41's immediately available power. They are replenished constantly by CT-41's currently equipped Generator.

High-Density Battery

The High-Density Battery is the Caretaker model's standard battery.

Ion Matrix

The Ion Matrix is a high capacity electrical storage medium developed by human scientists on Earth, and fabricated by Farsight colonists from specifications sent by the United Colonial Administration. CT-41 finds the Ion Matrix near the Farsight Colony's Reactor.

Photon Crystal

The Photon Crystal is a dark crystal capable of housing an large amount of energy in the form of trapped photons. CT-41 finds the Photon Crystal after it defeats the Crystal King.

Electron Well

The Electron Well is a Native energy store that can carry a huge amount of electrical energy. Along with trash and many worthless items, the Electron Well spills out of the burst Tumor once CT-41 destroys it and its various nodes.

God's Hoard

The God's Hoard is a uneven stony object capable of storing immense energy reserves. The Nanite Colony adapts the steaming Seed of #OLD_GOD into the God's Hoard.

Generators

Generators produce electrical enery consantly, filling up CT-41's currently equipped Battery.

Radioisotope Thermoelectric Generator

The Radioisotope Thermoelectric Generator is the Caretaker model's standard electrical generator.

Fusion Core

The Fusion Core is a high output Native electrical generator that CT-41 finds in the Geothermal Power Station.

Electron Conduit

The Electron Conduit is spacetime funnel that routes a large amount of power from a remote Stellar Harvester to a distant endpoint. CT-41 takes the Electron Conduit from the Repository.

Inferno Chamber

The Inferno Chamber is an experimental electrical generator that produces enormous power. CT-41 finds the Inferno Chamber in the wreckage of the Large Threat Suppressor.

God's Heart

The God's Heart is a flaming craggy stone capable of generating monstrous power. The Nanite Colony adapts the pulsing Seed of Ashtaroth into the God's Hoard.

Motors

Motors determine the torque and maximal rotational velocity of CT-41's wheels.

Umeme Motor

The Umeme Motor is the Caretaker model's standard motor.

Cyclone Drive

The Cyclone Drive is a high torque Native motor, capable of higher maximum rotational velocity than the Umeme Motor. CT-41 finds the Cyclone Drive in the Repair Shop.

Constant Rotor

The Constant Rotor is a Destroyer motor with extremeley high torque and uncapped maximum rotational velocity. Like all Destroyer items, it does not drain CT-41's battery. CT-41 finds the Constant Rotor in the Hangar.

Pistons

Pistons determine CT-41's maximum jumping impulse.

Spring Pistons

The Spring Pistons are the Caretaker model's standard pistons.

Hydraulic Pistons

The Hydraulic Pistons that CT-41 finds in the Hall of Relics allow it to jump to great height.

Pneumatic Pistons

The Pneumatic Pistons that CT-41 finds in the Refinery after taking the Commander's Rank card allow it to jump to extreme height.

Sensors

Sensors are objects that CT-41 takes from the Outsider's Watchers that patrol the Underground. Once the Sensors have been adapted, they alert CT-41 to places of interest that produce anomalous readings. Sensors indicate the direction of off-screen positive readings.

The Watchers are unlike normal bosses in that the do not attack CT-41, but they are highly elusive and evasive. Each time CT-41 destroys a Watcher, the next generation of Watcher will be more difficult to destroy. The order in which CT-41 destroys the Watchers does not matter.

Motion Sensor

The Motion Sensor detects the presence of lifeforms. CT-41 takes the Motion Sensor from a damaged Watcher near the Fuel Dump.

Electron Sensor

The Electron Sensor detects the presence negatively charged ions, or anions. CT-41 takes the Electron Sensor from a Watcher sent to the Fly Traps.

Infrared Sensor

The Infrared Sensor detects hotspots in walls, machinery and enemies. CT-41 takes the Infrared Sensor from a Watcher sent to the Quarry.

Radiation Sensor

The Radiation Sensor detects sources of high-energy radiation. CT-41 takes the Radiation Sensor from a Watcher sent to the Lower Ruins.

Other Items
Aerial Thrusters

The Aerial Thrusters are thrusters given to CT-41 by Vadim Kozlov. The Thrusters allow CT-41 to move horizontally while mid-air.

Nanite Colony

The Nanite Colony is a hive of self-replicating nanites that slowly repair CT-41 after it takes damage. Although they cannot repair its carbon-lattice brain, the nanites can keep CT-41 ready for exploration and combat unless it takes catastrophic damage. CT-41 receives the Colony as a gift from Dr. Ines Orozco.

As it explores Lumen 2, CT-41 finds many items and materials that were not designed to be use by it. The Nanite Colony can spend days, weeks, or years analyzing the items, and adapting them for compatibility with CT-41's systems.

Destroyer Kernel

The Destroyer Kernel is an item taken from the destroyed Custodian. Once CT-41 has the Destroyer Kernel in its possession, it can pass through Destroyer Gateways.

Materials

Materials do not help CT-41 by themselves, but can be used to improve many of CT-41's Tools. Many Native Tools grow in power as more materials are poured into them, while Kozlov's Toy changes in function when a new material is slotted into it. Most materials are found many times throughout the Underground, while the materials that enable Kozlov's Toy are only found once.

As materials were scarce, difficult, costly and time consuming to produce, and highly useful, they are typically well hidden or protected by their owners. Large amounts of Materials can be found in the Sunken Ship, in the Vault, and in the Prospecting Camp.

Each Material that CT-41 can find and use is listed next to the Tool it improves.

Item Strategies and Tricks

CT-41 uses the Volt Gun to arch lightning across several enemies and reach exposed wiring that is otherwise impossible to target.

CT-41 fires the Scattershot and the Particle Emitter simultaneously and continuously for maximal added velocity before and during a jump.

CT-41 shoots an object with the Mass Inhibitor, and the hits it with the Impact Cannon. The object flies away at high velocity, and when CT-41 is no longer shooting it with the Mass Inhibitor, its momentum rises dramatically.

CT-41 shoots an an enemy that typically self-destructs with the Medusa, and uses the resulting stony mass as a weight to press a switch, or as a platform for jumping.

CT-41 uses the Impact Cannon in tandem with its Aerial Thruster to work its way up very steep slopes or walls. With increased power generation, battery, shielding and armor, the vertical distance it can scale increases.

CT-41 uses the Mass Inhibitor in tandem with the Medusa to petrify large objects.

CT-41 uses the Corner Cutter and/or the Volatility Launcher to attack enemies from safe positions.
