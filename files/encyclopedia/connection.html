Connection
Overview

The Connection was a system of light bearing cables that linked Terminals throughout the Realm, allowing for rapid communication between individual Lumene Natives, and between Lineages. The Connection also allowed Natives at Terminals to access and confer with the Intelligence.

During the Hidden Age, the young Outsider used a hidden Terminal in the Badlands to communicate with other Natives of the Remnant, and the Engineers especially. It was via the Connection that he became educated in the physical sciences and mathematics, and began to impress the Engineers with novel theorems. Whe he revealed his Lineage to the Engineers over the Connection, they asked the Rulers to grant him a special exemption to leave his Locus, and join the Engineers in the Hearth.

Engineers designed and installed hardware for the Connection before the Destruction. They chose to lay physical communication lines due to the difficulty of wireless information transfer through the dense rock of the Undergound. After a Vermin infestation began to drain power produced at the Geothermal Power Station, the Connection and the Terminals ceased to function.

Truth II

During the events of Truth II, CT-41 may access the Connection once it has restored power to the Realm, and has found the Outsider. CT-41 can use the Connection to operate certain machinery, remotely or otherwise, and speak to the Intelligence.
