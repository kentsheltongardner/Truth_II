Age
Overview

The Age was the broadest time period codified by Lumene historians. Ages were delineated by cosmological, geological, evolutionary or historical events rather than any regular time period, and as such were distinct from Turns, Lives, and Cycles. The history studied by Native chroniclers drew heavily from lived experience from the Chain of Memory, as well as from geological, fossil, archaeological and written records.

Lumene Ages
Inanimate Age

The Inanimate Age began at the Big Bang, and ended when a meteor struck the surface of Lumen 2, producing the Fortuna Crater. It was conjectured by some Lumene Healers and Engineers that the meteor strike shattered the planet's crust, and allowed simple organisms from the Upper Mantle to begin moving upward. Others believed that the meteor carried organic chemicals that flourished into life. Regardless, it was near the time of the impact that fossils of simple organisms began to form in sediments near the Fortuna Crater.

Genetic Age

The Genetic Age began with the appearance of simple lifeforms in the crust of Lumen 2, and ended when the mind of the Primordion developed in an ancient Native ancestor living in the Womb. It was within the Seed of this creature that the Chain of Memory began to form.

Aquatic Age

The Aquatic Age began when the mind of the Primordion developed, and allowed its bearer to become the dominant lifeform in the shallow regions of the Sea and the pools surrounding it. The Aquatic Age ended when the first Proto-Native emerged from the waters, and began to move on land.

Emergent Age

The Emergent Age began as Proto-Natives moved to land, and the mind of the Simpleton formed. This new creature began to create tunnels and burrows, and aided the Old Gods in their hunts. The Emergent Age ended with the Pre-Tribal Extinction, when a coronal mass ejection from Lumen killed much of the life on Lumen 2.

Tribal Age

The Tribal Age began when the Old Gods died or entered Dormancy, and the Lumene Natives ascended to became the dominant lifeform of the Underground. The mind of the Luminary formed within the Common Ancestor, and different populations of Natives became isolated and formed distinct cultures. The Tribal Age ended with the Tribal Wars, and the Unification.

Enlightened Age

The Enlightened Age began with the Unification of the Kindred, and ended with the first permanent dwellings being built on the surface of Lumen 2. During the Enlightened Age Lumene Natives developed industrial technologies that allowed them to sustain larger populations, and survive the hostile conditions on their planet's surface.

Planetary Age

The Planetary Age began as Lumene Natives began to cover the surface of their world. Lumene civilization spread outwards from the Fortuna Crater, and settlements of the Kindred were found all across Lumen 2. The Planetary Age ended when Lumene Explorers escaped the relatively weak gravity well of their homeworld, and established the first permanent base on Nox.

Stellar Age

The Stellar Age began as Lumene Natives began to visit and colonize their moon and the other planets in the Lumen system. The Age ended when Explorer Probes were discovered by Destroyer sensor stations, and latent Destroyer constructs activated to bring the Destruction to all worlds populated by Lumene Natives.

Hidden Age

The Hidden Age began with the Destruction, when the Remnant gathered to the Womb. The Age ended when Thosmodeme's Laborers sealed the Rulers and Executors in their Locus, ushering in the Subjugation.

Dark Age

The Dark Age, also called the Last Age, began with the Subjugation. After the Slaughter no Native chroniclers remained to codify any new Ages, and this age was considered to have been the last.
