Alo Nutre
Overview

Alo Nutre was an Enhanced Human who served as Diplomatic Liason aboard the Paralus. Alo Nutre was killed, along with the rest of the Paralus' crew, when Voronash left the Outsider's Seed and manifested himself as a monster of darkness.
