Chayot Ha Kodesh
Overview

The Chayot ha Kodesh was an Angel that guarded the door to the Laboratory, called by him and his brethren the 'Holy of Holies'. The Chayot ha Kodesh was the most recent offspring of the Outsider, and the Angel most similar to his father in form and ability.

Truth II

During the events of Truth II, CT-41 confronts and defeats the Chayot ha Kodesh. After entering the Laboratory it does not see another living being until it enters the chamber of the Prime Altar, and confronts the Outsider, and Voronash.
