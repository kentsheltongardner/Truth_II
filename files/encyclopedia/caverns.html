Caverns
Overview

The Caverns were a network of naturally formed subterranean chambers and tunnels, accessible from the surface of Lumen 2 via a fissure at the western edge of the Fortuna Crater. They housed a typical Lumene Shallows ecosystem, which depended a great deal on the rains and the Migration that followed the dusk and dawn seasons of the Turn. In the deepest and easternmost chambers of the Caverns some Native architecture was present, but it was heavily damaged by the Destruction, exposed to the elements, and overrun by primitive life.

The Caverns were the hunting grounds of a solitary Beast which killed Ensign Nicholas Ormeil on #DATE, in a highly uncharacteristic attack. Ormeil left behind a Flashlight and recording and transmission equipment in the upper portions of the Caverns.

Truth II

During the events of Truth II, CT-41 enters the Caverns, recovers the Flashlight, and kills the Beast. In the Beast's Den CT-41 finds Ormeil's headless skeleton, his PDR, and his Ensign's Rank Card.
