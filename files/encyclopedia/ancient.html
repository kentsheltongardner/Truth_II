Ancient
Overview

The Ancients were Lumene megafauna that lived on the surface of Lumen 2 and in the Underground during the Aquatic and Emergent Ages. 'Ancient' was an umbrella term used to refer to many different species, including the Old Gods.

During the Pre-Tribal Extinction, almost all Ancients went extinct. Lumene Natives remembered the forms of many of the Ancients, as well as emotions of awe, reverence and terror associated with them.

Truth II

During the events of Truth II, CT-41 finds the fossilized remains of several Ancients at the Fossil Site and elsewhere throughout the Underground. It also finds several Old Gods in Dormancy, including one killed in Dormancy during the Destruction.
