Creator
Overview

The Creator is an entity more powerful than all but the most potent Singularities. Indeed he was once very nearly a Singularity himself, before his purpose was made manifest. In spite of his immense power, he does not rule over his Creation as a tyrant. Instead, the Creator freely admits any willing entity into his Creation, with certain conditions. The Creator's strength and scope increase as entities join themselves to him willingly, becoming unique Selves while obeying his Law.

Truth II

During the events of Truth II, CT-41 catches a glimpse of the Creator's will as it speaks with Kozlov in the Hermit's Cave.
