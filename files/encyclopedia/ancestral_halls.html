Ancestral Halls
Overview

The Ancestral Halls were a complex of beautifully decorated chambers near the Quarry, wherein shrines were dedicated to the Progenitors of the various Native Lineages. Construction of the Ancestral Halls began during the third Turn of the Enlightened Age, when the Tribal Wars was only a memory and the Kindred had acheived a stable society.

Each shrine within the Ancestral Halls contained a statue of a Progenitor. The Halls were arranged as a branching structure of chambers, from the Common Ancestor at the base, to the Ancestral Lineages, to the High Lineages at the top.

No chamber was set aside for the Laborer in the Ancestral Halls, since at the time of their construction the Progenitor of the Laborers had not yet been discovered. When the Laborers were admitted into the Kindred, it was difficult to add their shrine to the Halls, since their Lineage diverged from all others before the birth of the Common Ancestor. As such, the Laborers constructed their own shrine, at the center of the pool where their Proto-Native ancestor slept during his long Dormancy.

Truth II

During the events of Truth II, CT-41 defeats the Bronze Executor that stands guard at the entrance to the Ancestral Halls. After moving through the various shrine chambers, CT-41 finds the dead Outsider and the Necrolith perched atop the Explorer's Shrine. The Commentary converses with CT-41 from within the Necrolith, and joins it in its journey.

Soon thereafter, CT-41 discovers it is trapped in the Explorer's shrine chamber. It finds an Impulse Cannon that had been used to deface the Explorer's shrine, and blasts apart cracked stone in the wall of the chamber to escape.
