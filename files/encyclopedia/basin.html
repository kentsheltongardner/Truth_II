Basin
Overview

The Basin, where the Native Rulers lived and worked, was the deepest Locus of the Lumene Underground. It was situated near the Upper Mantle, west of the Hearth, and contained various administrative, intelligence, and law enforcement facilities in addition to Native residences.

During the events of Truth II, CT-41 gains access to the Rulers' exclusive portion of Basin after defeating the three Greater Fragments of Thosmodeme, and moving through the Factory and into the Boiling Lake. It is from the Basin that CT-41 is able to move into the Holy City.

Areas
Boiling Lake

The Boiling Lake was an inhospitable region of the Deep, near the westernmost Magma Chamber, where drainage from the Sea and the Womb accumulated. Owing to its proximity to the Magma Chamber, the Lake was extremely hot, and its waters were lethal to unprotected Lumene Natives.

Shortly before the Subjugation, and under Thosmodeme's direction, Lumene Laborers began digging a tunnel from the Boiling Lake to the Rulers' Locus. When he deemed that the time was right, Thosmodeme instructed his Laborers to seal and flood the Locus, and destroy the Rulers and Executors. His plan succeeded, and the Rulers were boiled alive in their home.

During the events of Truth II, CT-41 passes through the Boiling Lake en route to the Rulers' Locus, taking care to avoid long term exposure to its damaging and corrosive waters, and avoiding the hardy and dangerous lifeforms that had evolved to thrive in it.

Data Center

The Data Center was a digital library wherein the Rulers housed all records of the Kindred. At the heart of the Data Center was the Intelligence, a powerful mind arising from a web of interconnected Native Seeds. All Terminals throughout the Realm were connected directly to the Data Center, allowing point-to-point communication, and access to archival data and the Intelligence.

Factory

The Factory was a large facility at the eastern end of the Basin, where the majority of the Remnant's material goods were produced. The various production lines in the Factory were supplied with raw materials from the Mineral Separation Plant and other sources throughout the Underground.

Shortly after the Destruction, Native Engineers began to renovate a run-down Factory near the Magma Chambers so that it could serve the Remnant. When their work was finished, the Factory began to produce many different industrial and residential goods, and its output greatly eased the suffering of the Remnant.

By the end of the Hidden Age, Laborers comprised the majority of the Factory's work force. Though the Factory was declared to be a communal property, as a practical matter only a relatively few Rulers and Engineers acted as its owners and administrators.

During the events of Truth II, CT-41 navigates the Factory's production lines, and finds the Resonator.

Fortress

The Fortress was a heavily fortified area at the entrance to the Basin, where Executors were manufactured, assembled, and housed. The Fortress contained surveilance equipment and stationary weaponry, though after the flooding of the Basin much of this machinery became corroded and was no longer functional.

During the Tribal Wars, the Fortress was the stronghold of the Nurturers and Philosophers. In it they stored all the provision they could, in preparation for siege. The Fortress and the western gate of the Assembly Hall were the lines beyond which the Adventurers were never able to advance. The Fortress contains a museum wing, with several well preserved chambers from the time of the Wars. It also features refuges, hideaways and escape routes that were forgotten or never filled in after hostilities ceased.

Gallery

The Gallery was an area where the Rulers housed and displayed significant historical artifacts, natural phenomena collected from the Underground, and notable artistic creations. The display pieces included:

An arrangement of skulls, including those of an Old God, a Beast, a Native, and a Pet.

An Explorer Probe and other spacefaring technologies.

A painted mural of the distorted surface of Lumen 2 encompassing Lumen, Nox, and the Lumene starscape.

Primitive Glowstone idols.

Large and small gemstones on multi-tiered displays.

Artisticly formed silicate growth specimens.

Surface rocks, including meteor fragments.

Anomalous animal specimens, preserved in tanks or cylinders.

Traditional Native clothing and ancient tools.

An ornate bas relief map of the Realm, divided into its Loci.

A large, broken aquarium.

During the Flood, many of the loosely mounted display were destroyed or knocked about. During the events of Truth II, CT-41 finds the Gallery completely flooded and overrun by Lumene organisms. The museum has itself become a relic of historical circumstance.

Palace

The Palace was a sprawling living area once peopled by the Lumene Rulers, beautified to the best of the Rulers' ability. It contained numerous habitations, and council chambers where Rulers and invited guests discussed Realm politics and law in great depth. During the Flood the Palace was inundated by boiling water, and its inhabitants were killed.

Vault

The Vault was a high security chamber where Native Rulers stockpiled Material stabilization vials and other valuables.

Warehouse

The Warehouse was a series of chambers, adjacent to the Factory, where the Rulers stockpiled manufactured goods before distribution.
