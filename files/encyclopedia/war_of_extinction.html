Directive
Overview

The War of Extinction was the name given to IDF's centuries long conflict with the Destroyers. Although humanity proved to be a particularly dificult challenge, the Destroyers ultimately won the War, and razed Earth and her colonies just as they had destroyed thousands of other worlds.

After the IDF's collapse, groups of humans scattered across the galaxy, prompting the Destroyers to begin a methodical and effective campaign of tracking and eradicating human survivors.

Truth II

During the events of Truth II, CT-41 learns of the current state of the War from UCA officials, from Jusuf Manigault, and from the Oracle.
