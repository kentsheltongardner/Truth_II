Dark Room
Overview

The Dark Room was a chamber west of the Chemical Disposal Area, where a Proto-Native predecessor of the Common Ancestor lived during the Pre-Tribal Extinction. It was the setting of the Shame, a painful recollection in the Chain of Memory of most Lumene Natives.

After a coronal mass ejection impacted Lumen 2 at the end of the Emergent Age, many surface species quickly went extinct. The effects of the extinction were felt more gradually in the Underground. The balance of life was dirupted, food became scarce, and many creatures died or went into Dormancy. The Dark Room was a well hidden chamber in which a Proto-Native took refuge, occasionally emerging to hunt and scavenge in darkness.

One of the Old Gods eventually learned of the Proto-Native, and saw it return to the Dark Room. It entered the chamber, and found the Proto-Native and its young. The Old God was starving, and the Proto-Native had no offering for it. The Old God was weakened, and might not have been willing to risk injury in a fight against an adult Proto-Native. However, in the Dark Room, the Proto-Native chose to dims its lights and flee, leaving the Old God to feast on its children. The Proto-Native never returned to the Dark Room, since the Old God knew its location.

In later ages, memories of the Dark Room caused Natives of the High Lineages great discomfort. The chamber was not visited, and was never claimed or developed by any Lineage.

Truth II

During the events of Truth II, CT-41 is able to open the passage to the Dark Room after it finds the Impact Cannon. Inside the Dark Room the Outsider grants CT-41 a Vision of the Shame.
