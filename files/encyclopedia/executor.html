Executor
Overview

Executors were a class of powerful, semi-artificial beings ideated, designed and constructed by Native Rulers, Engineers and Healers. They were created to assume the role of the Warrior after the Sacrifice.

Executors possessed powerful robotic bodies, each controlled by an electronically stimulated Warrior's Seed. The robotic bodies were designed in imitation of Lumene Native Warriors, so that the Seeds could easily interface with them.

Executors were manufactured and garrisoned in the Fortress, using the Seeds of Warriors taken from the Treasury and the floor of the Arena. Executors were loyal to the Rulers and the Intelligence only. Their duties were to defend the Realm from Beasts and Rogues, and to enforce the mandates handed down by the Rulers.

Truth II

During the events of Truth II, CT-41 encounters several unique Executors throughout the Underground. These Executors do not recognize CT-41, and regard it as a threat.
