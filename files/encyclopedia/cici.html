CICI
Overview

CICI was the name given by Ensign Nicholas Ormeil to the Argo's central computer. CICI was in charge of coordinating all computer subsystems, processing queries, and communicating results to Farsight Expedition personnel. During the construction of the Farsight Colony, CICI was integrated with many Colony systems, including its power grid.

Immediately after Cobb's Mutiny, Cobb himself cleared CICI's memory banks, to prevent other parties from discovering the fate of the colonists. During the period of time between the Mutiny and the emergency summons that awakened CT-41 from sleep mode, CICI maintained an Event Log of all major happenings in the Colony.

Truth II

During the events of Truth II, CICI helps to direct CT-41, offers analyses of its acquisitions, and alerts CT-41 when she receives communications from Earth.

When the Outsider damages the Communications Bunker after sending his message to Earth, CICI notifies CT-41, who returns to the Colony to investigate. Per intruder protocol, CICI places the Colony in lockdown, and CT-41 must find a novel way into the Colony.
