Anomaly
Overview

An Anomaly was a Lumene organism that differed greatly in appearance and/or behavior from its closest relatives. Anomalous lifeforms almost always evolved during long periods of isolation. Anomalous Lumene Natives were called Rogues. Those Rogues that diverged radically from the Natives of the Kindred often posed a serious threat to other Natives. Part of the Warriors' mandate was to protect Native society from Anomalous Natives.

Truth II

During the events of Truth II, CT-41 encounters many Anomalous creatures, which often prove to be more dangerous than common lifeforms. Defeating these Anomalies yields a large bounty of optimization for CT-41's lasers. Anomalous creatures will occasionally drop items or materials when killed.
