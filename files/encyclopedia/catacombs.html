Catacombs
Overview

The Catacombs were a network of meandering caverns and tunnels in the western Deep, where members of an extinct tribe of primitive Lumene Natives called Worshippers entombed themselves. These Natives worshipped the gods of the Underworld, and buried themselves as near to that warm, eternal realm as possible.

By the time of the Tribal Wars, the Catacombs were already ancient beyond reckoning, and unknown to the Kindred. Of all Natives of the Kindred, only the Outsider learned of the Catacombs' existence.

After the Singularity entered Creation, it fled from the activity and noise of the Native Loci, and settled in the silent Catacombs to study Creation's Law and take shape. There it learned to read and interact with the Seeds of dead Natives in their silent tombs. Due to this formative experience, Thosmodeme and Voronash were able to perceive and influence the minds of most natural lifeforms.

Truth II

During the events of Truth II, CT-41 takes refuge in the Catacombs as it flees from Voronash after the battle in the Throneroom. Voronash is unable to pursue the much smaller CT-41 through the Catacombs' small and winding tunnels.
