Clifftop
Overview

The Clifftop was a flat, dusty plateau on the eastern rim of the Fortuna Crater. Farsight Expedition Marines and work crews scaled the Crater wall and cleared the land in the weeks after the Argo touched down on Lumen 2. Once Farsight personnel completed the Surface Access Elevator, construction of Clifftop facilities such as the Barracks, Transmission Array, Hangar and Launch Silo began in earnest.

Though the surface life had largely rebounded from the Destruction by the time the Argo arrived on Lumen 2, huge dust clouds still circulated across the world. As such, the door to the Surface Access Elevator became obstructed once there were no colonists to attend to it.

Truth II

During the events of Truth II, CT-41 is initially unable to use the Surface Access Elevator to reach the Clifftop, due to a massive build up of hardened dust against the doors. With the Captain's Rank Card it receives from Jusuf Manigault, CT-41 is able to open the Hangar and move out onto the surface. Once there, it can clear the sediments away from the Surface Access Elevator using the Resonator.

If CT-41 leaps from the Clifftop with a running start and while using the Boost Jet and Maneuvering Thrusters, CT-41 can land on the dorsal surface of the Paralus. With the Electron Bore it can burn through weak patches of metal plating on the vessel's dorsal surface. This is the only way for CT-41 to enter the Paralus once its cargo lift is retracted into the body of the ship.
