Destroyer Wreck
Overview

The Destroyer Wreck was an enormous, mangled segment of a Destroyer Gateway found on the surface of Lumen 2, east of the Fortuna Crater. The Destroyer Wreck and many other such Gateway segments crashed to Lumen 2 from orbit after the Warriors' Sacrifice. The Farsight Colonists explored and studied the Destroyer Wreck, as it was the closest and most interesting Destroyer .
