Lower Ruins
Overview

The Lower Ruins were an isolated web of Native dwellings beneath the Refuge and west of the Holy City where the Worshippers lived during the Tribal Age. The Lower Ruins were closer to the Underworld and the main vent of the Volcano than any other ancient Native ruins.

The Worshippers who lived in the Deep Ruins adapted to the heat of their home, and were visibly distinct from other Native populations. The Lower Ruins contained no clear archaeological clues as to the final fate of the Worshippers.

Truth II

During the events of Truth II, CT-41 escaped from Voronash by fleeing into and hiding in the Lower Ruins.
