Pumping Station
Overview

The Pumping Station was a Native facility west of the Sea that once pumped water to the Badlands, the Desolation, and the Lumene surface. Large diameter pipes ran along and then away from the Openings to carry this water. After the Destruction the Pumping Station only served the Explorers and the machinery they managed in the Badlands.

Truth II

During the events of Truth II, CT-41 enters the Pumping Station once it has the Hydraulic Pistons. After navigating through the Station's pools and tubing, and defeating the Serpent, CT-41 acquires the Turbine.
