Common Ancestor
Overview

The Common Ancestor was the first true Lumene Native, distinguished from its relatives by the mind of the Luminary that it carried. It evolved in the pre-technological Tribal Age, before the species branched into the Ancestral Lineages.

When the Common Ancestor acheived rationality, it struggled with the question of its proper place in the world, and with many difficult memories it had inherited, such as the Shame. Many Natives believed that the offspring of the Common Ancestor split into new Lineages as each found separate answers to these moral and existential questions. Others believed that the divergence of Lineages was attributable to the distinct physical environments in which early Natives lived.

The Laborers were not descended from the Common Ancestor, but were nevertheless admitted into the Kindred as a Lineage. This admission was an irritant to the more technically minded among the Kindred.

Truth II

During the events of Truth II, CT-41 sees an enormous shrine dedicated to the Common Ancestor as it enters the Ancestral Halls.
