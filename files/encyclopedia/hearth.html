Hearth
Overview

Areas
Accelerator

The Accelerator was one of the crowning scientific achievements of the Lumene Natives, constructed and improved over many Turns well into the Stellar and Hidden Ages. Huge amounts of energy from the Geothermal Power Station were used to accelerate and collide particles at incredible velocities, producing volumes of data about the sub-atomic world.

In addition to performing scientific research, Engineers working with the Accelerator also used it to build up stockpiles of exotic matter, which was stored in containment units near the Accelerator itself. Since these materials could be stored in small spaces and had high practical and scientific value, they were used as a reserve currency in Lumene finance.

During his career working with the Engineers, the Outsider spent much of his time at the Accelerator, devising new theoretical models of the quantum universe, and designing improvements and new machinery to test his hypotheses.

During the events of Truth II, CT-41 finds the Particle Emitter at the eastern end of the Accelerator facility. CT-41 must be accompanied by the Stargazer before it can pass through the Engineers' Locus Doors and enter the Accelerator.

Condensation Vault

Condensation Vaults were high, narrow chambers where steam coming from the turbines in the Geothermal Power Station was passively cooled, condensing into water that then flowed to the Reservoir.

Control Center

The Control Center was a chamber above the heat of the Geothermal Power Station where electrical output from the Power Station was monitored, and production was throttled to meet the demands placed on the power grid by Native facilities throughout the Underground. The Control Center contained many displays, a Terminal giving access to the Connection, and controls to manage the flow of water through the Power Station's tubing. Not far from the Control Center was a hidden chamber containing an Altar.

Domiciles

The Domiciles were a compact arrangement of Native living quarters in the Restricted Area. These dwellings were highly space efficient and modern, and were located very closed to the Engineers' working spaces.

Power Distribution Room

The Power Distribution Room was a compartment in the Geothermal Power Station where voltage was manipulated and routed to various Native facilities throughout the Underground.

Prototyping Labs

The Prototyping Labs were a collection of chambers in the Restricted Area where Native Engineers conducted scientific tests and fabricated experimental devices. The Golden Executor prototype was developed in the Prototyping Labs, along with a host of other robotic and cybernetic devices.

Repository

The Repository was an area deep in the Restricted Area where Native Engineers and Rulers stored and studied all Destroyer artifacts collected by the Remnant after the Sacrifice. Experts from other Lineages were often invited to inspect the artifacts under the Rulers' supervision. The Repository contained several chambers where scientists conducted material and functional analysis.

The most important piece of Destroyer wreckage studied in the Repository was the Custodian, a damaged maintenance construct. Over several Turns of study, the Linguist learned to pry information out of the Custodian, and discovered much of the Destroyers' nature, history, and mission.

Restricted Area

The Restricted Area was a portion of the Hearth accessible only by Engineers, certain Rulers, and invited guests. It encompassed the Domiciles, the Prototyping Labs, the Molecular Assembler, and the Repository. Originally protected by the Engineers' Locus Doors, it was later sealed shut by Laborers during the Subjugation.
