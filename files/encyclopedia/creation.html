Creation
Overview

Creation is a finite yet ever growing region within the Real that is claimed by the Creator, and where he enforces his Law upon the willing. It is a haven against the incomprehensible terrors and insatiable Singularities that haunt the Outside. Within Creation time and space expand, and Law protects the freedom and distinctness of all Selves. Creation's walls are mighty, and stand firm against those who would destroy it, or enter unbidden.

Before any Self may be admitted into Creation, it must first accept the Creator's Law. Only entities that are willing enter Creation and are made Selves, accepting Creation's responsibilities and enjoying its privileges. Like a Singularity, Creation grows as new entities enter it. However, while Singularities become increasingly dense and ultimately unstable, Creation is a kingdom that expands forever.

Truth II

During the events of Truth II, CT-41 exits and re-enters Creation countless times as it uses the Outsider's Altars to move around Creation to different points in space and time.
