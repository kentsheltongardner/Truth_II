Volcano
Overview

The Volcano was a mountain, west of the Fortuna Crater, that was once connected to the Underworld via its main vent. When the Argo arrived on Lumen 2, the Volcano had been dormant for #NUMBER years. The Volcano's main vent and its various side vents and sills had all cooled. One of the Volcano's sills connected to the Laccolith, where Lumene Natives quarried their stone.

Truth II

During the events of Truth II, CT-41 climbs up the Volcano's Mountainside, eventually reaching the Hermit's Cave near the summit.
