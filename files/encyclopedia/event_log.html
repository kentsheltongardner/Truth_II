Event Log
Overview

During the events of Truth II, CT-41 is able to access CICI's event log at computer terminals throughout the Farsight Colony.

Although Otis Cobb cleared the Event Log before leaving the Colony, it still contains entries describing all major events that occurred in the Colony after Cobb's Mutiny.

Entries

#DATE #TIME: "The current crop is overdue for harvest. I have attempted to notify the greenhouse staff, but have received no response."

#DATE #TIME: "Floodwaters have filled several of the lower shafts in the uraninite mine, and the structural strength of some of the shafts may be compromised. I have attempted to notify mining crews of the possibility of collapse, but have received no response."

#DATE #TIME: "Accumulated dust has rendered the surface access elevator inoperable. I have attempted to notify maintenance crews of the damage, but have received no response."

#DATE #TIME: "Lumen is about to set, and temperatures are dropping rapidly. I have attempted to notify all colonial personnel of the danger, but have received no response."
