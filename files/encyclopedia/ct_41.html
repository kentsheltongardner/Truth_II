CT-41
Overview

CT-41, serial number CT-CA-00000041, was one of several Caretaker Colonial Assistants created to accompany the Farsight Expedition. Of the several Caretakers sent to Lumen 2, CT-41 was the only one to be assembled and activated. CT-41 was called 'Caretaker' or 'CT' by most human expedition members and Natives.

Truth II

CT-41's choices and experiences, from the moment it is activated until it moves onto the Prime Altar, are Truth II.
