Boiling Waters
Overview

The Boiling Waters were an inhospitable region of the Deep, near the westernmost Magma Chamber, where drainage from the Sea and the Womb accumulated. Owing to its proximity to the Magma Chamber, the Waters were extremely hot, and were lethal to unprotected Lumene Natives.

Shortly before the Subjugation, and under Thosmodeme's direction, Lumene Laborers began digging a tunnel from the Boiling Waters to the Rulers' Locus. When he deemed that the time was right, Thosmodeme instructed his Laborers to seal and flood the Locus, and destroy the Rulers and Executors. His plan succeeded, and the Rulers were boiled alive in their home.

Truth II

During the events of Truth II, CT-41 passes through the Boiling Waters en route to the Rulers' Locus, taking care to avoid long term exposure to its damaging and corrosive waters, and avoiding the hardy and dangerous lifeforms that had evolved to thrive in them.
