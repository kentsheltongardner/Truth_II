United Colonial Administration
Overview

The United Colonial Administration, called 'UCA' or simply 'the Administration', was an international organization, formed in #YEAR, that regulated human colonization of extraterrestrial worlds. At its inception the Administration worked to equitably partition real estate on near-Earth objects, and granted colonization and mineral rights to applicant nations and corporations. It later regulated the colonization of extra-solar worlds.

With the development of the Rabinowitz Process in #YEAR, and the subsequent availability of antimatter as fuel, manned interstellar travel was brought into the realm of possibiity. In #YEAR the Administration deployed the Large Interstellar Surveyor Telescope array, and began to exhaustively catalog potentially habitable exoplanets in the Orion Arm of the Milky Way. Soon afterward several proximal Earth-like planets were discovered, and applicants presented offers to the Administration for colonial rights.

The Jannah expedition was the first interstellar colonial venture to be approved by the UCA. Shortly afterward several other extra-solar colonial licenses were issued as LIST continued to discover habitable exoplanets. Before the first manned interstellar missions set out, the Administration created the UST standard and began its broadcast.

In #YEAR, after upgrades to the LIST array increased its search radius, scientists discovered artificial patterns on the surfaces of three planets in the Lumen system. This knowledge was kept classified, and the United Colonial Administration, in cooperation with several large governments on Earth and Mars, began preparations for the Farsight Expedition.

In response to later Destroyer aggression, the Colonial Administration and all its assets were acquired by the Solar Navy to form the IDF in #YEAR UST.

Truth II

During the events of Truth II, the United Colonial Administration maintains long distance communication with CT-41 for the first #NUMBER years of its mission. In addition to issuing orders, the UCA sends updates on the status of Earth and the conflict with the Destroyers.
