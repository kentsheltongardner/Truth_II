Yamato Robotics
Overview

The Yamato-Ruach Robotics Corporation (YRRC), more commonly called 'Yamato Robotics', was created in #YEAR by the acquisition of the Israeli venture 'Ruach Information Systems' by the Japanese company 'Yamato Electronics'. The young company began as a robotics parts supplier for a variety of industrial clients, but Yamato's leadership had higher ambitions.

In the early #DECADE Yamato Robotics entered the market for domestic and labor assistance automata, and joined the race to produce an Embodied Artificial General Intelligence. After more than a decade of research and development, and with valuable input from designers such as Jessica Arai, YRRC produced a commercial EAGI, a labor and maintenance robot called the Caretaker.
