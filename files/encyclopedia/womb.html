Womb
Overview

The Womb was a network of warm, watery caverns beneath and to the west of the Sea, where the primitive ancestors of the Lumene Natives evolved. The Womb was considered sacred by the Natives, and was Sanctified by the Rulers during the Enlightened Age. The Womb served as a refuge to many Natives of the Remnant during the Destruction, and during the Hidden Age, surviving Natives returned to and beautified their ancient Loci that surrounded it.

The Womb served as a large park and conservation area for the Natives, and was a central area between the Loci where the lesser lifeforms of the Lumene Underground were permitted to flourish relatively undisturbed. It was in the Womb that a Farmer of the Remnant discovered a Proto-Native that had wakened from Dormancy during the Destruction. It was from this Proto-Native that the Laborer's Lineage descended.

After the Uprising and the Slaughter, the Womb became the Domain of Uruthmu, a Greater Fragment of Thosmodeme. In the fertile pools of the Womb Uruthmu bred Servants for Thosmodeme, and fed them with thick Flow from the Dark Origin.

Truth II

During the events of Truth II, CT-41 enters the Womb from the Sunken Ship at the bottom of the Sea, and navigates through its increasingly congested and defiled pools. CT-41 destroys the Dark Origin before passing through the lowest portions of the Abyssal Trench, and confronting Uruthmu in his Sanctum.
