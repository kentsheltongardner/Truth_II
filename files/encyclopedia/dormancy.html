Dormancy
Overview

Dormancy was a state of metabolic stasis into which many aerobic Lumene organisms entered when atmospheric conditions were unfavorable for respiration. Certain organisms such as the Old Gods buried themselves before entering Dormancy, while others such as Lumene Natives secreted a toxic, resinous shell around themselves. On the surface of Lumen 2, many creatures who entered dormancy went into near deathlike states at the coming of the frigid night or the burning day, emerging only when a Migration neared.

During the Pre-Tribal Extinction a young Proto-Native went into Dormacy in a hidden cavern in the Womb, and lay there undisturbed until the Destruction. Native Farmers found it when it awakened, and together with the Healers they encouraged its growth and learning. Descendants of the once dormant Proto-Native became the Lineage of the Laborer.

Periodic vs Traumatic Dormancy

Truth II

During the events of Truth II, CT-41 encounters many creatures in Dormancy on the surface of Lumen 2 and throughout the Underground. Of particular note are the sleeping Old Gods and the Tribesman.
