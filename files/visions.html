Visions
Overview

As CT-41 explores Lumen 2, its Companions occasionally directly communicate their experiences to it via Visions. Rather than speaking to CT-41, the Companions often show it events, overlaid onto the current scene, as they witnessed them. The visions generally have a ghostly, translucent aspect and ethereal sound, as the recollections of the Companions are not perfect in their details. Once a Vision is finished, its actors and props fade away.

A Vision is only displayed and heard once. However, as CT-41 gains new Companions, it will receive new Visions in areas it has already explored.

Visions
Assembly Hall

Boiling Lake

In the passageway between the Boiling Lake and the Fortress, the Follower grants CT-41 a vision of Laborers mining through the crust toward the Boiling Lake.

Dark Room

In the Dark Room, the Outsider grants CT-41 a vision of a cowering Proto-Native standing between its young and an Old God, before dimming its lights and fleeing.

Lower Root Fields

In the Lower Root Fields, the Groundskeeper grants CT-41 a vision of the Faithful tending the Deep Roots. Advanced machinery is conspicuously absent.
